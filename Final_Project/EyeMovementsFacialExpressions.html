Sama
Eyes kept absolutely still

Alokita
Rolling of eye-balls in a circular pattern

Ullokita
Looking upwards

Saachi
Looking through the corner of the eyes

Pralokita
Moving eyes from side to side

Avalokita
Looking down

Nimilita
Focusing eyes toward the heart and keeping them half shut

Anuvrita
Quick movement of the eyes up and down
