General Info
Bharatanatyam is a dance form popular in South India.
A Bharatanatyam performance includes a combination of expressions,
eye movements, hand gestures, and footwork.

The site will explain the meaning of specific dances and specific movements.

Further, it will detail the important aspects of proper Bharatanatyam steps.
