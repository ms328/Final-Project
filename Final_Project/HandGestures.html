Pataka

Tripataka
Pataka: when the fingers are all straight and thumb is bent

Tripathaka: the ring finger is bent from Pathaka posture

Mayura: the thumb is touching the first finger while the other fingers
are straight
Ardhachandra: from Pataka the thumb is seperated and out straight

Kapitta: the thumb is touching the middle of the first finger,
which is bent while the other fingers are pressed against the palm

Katakamukha: the thumb is stretched to touch the first finger and
the middle finger while the other two fingers are stretched and separated

Chandrakala: from Ardhachandra positions, the little, ring and middle
fingers are pressed against the palm
